About tACC

the Affective Computing Company develops software products and services that influence, recognize, interpret, process, and simulate human emotion. But, what does that mean? And, what’s the purpose?

Groups and Teams

The H2H Platform builds lasting bonds between groups and teams. This could refer to an employer and employess, a doctor and patiens, a teacher and students - any relationship that relies on continued growth and trust.

Individuals

The H2H Paltform helps individuals understand how their situations affect them. This understanding results in resilient indivduals who make confident decisions and take the right actions.

At the center of it all is the Holy Grail: Self-Awareness. At tACC, our motto is simple.Understand yourself. Feel better. Live better.

A Company Timeline

The Affective Computing Company has come a long way, starting in our founder’s attic, moving to a basement lab, to our newest digs in the East Liberty neighborhood of Pittsburgh. We built a great product along the way, too! Check out some of the highlights below.

Admin and User Portals

Introduce first version of Administrator and User Portals. These allow clients and users to view, analyze, and trend data, and take action!

 November 18th 2014

THRIVE Beta - Test Concludes

6-Month THRIVE beta-test with Bayer MaterialScience concludes. The result is 180,000 unique data points for the client related to Positivity, Communication and Contribution, both in the moment, and over time.

 October 13th 2014

Response Coding

Ability to create custom Coding Schemes within the platform. Clients can create “Class Codes” and assign as many as they would like to question responses. These give the responses meaning.

 July 30th 2014

SMS Capability

Experiences can be delivered via text messages, enabling personal and convenient interactions, and the collection of more honest data.

 June 20th, 2014

Custom User Feedback Screens

Enable Experiences to end with a custom feedback screen, showing users things like progress and comparisons.

June 8th, 2014

THRIVE Kick-Off

The H2H Platform starts delivering it’s first experiences to Bayer MaterialScience. Employees will interact with the platform daily, for no more than 60 seconds each time. We’ll focus on Positivity, Communication and Contribution.

 May 19th 2014

Branding Customization

The introduction of styleheets allow our clients to customize various aspects of the platform with their own branding, such as colors and logos.

 April 14th 2014

Our 1st Product - THRIVE!

tACC introduces our first product, THRIVE, to Bayer MaterialSciences. A new take on Employee Engagement, they agree to a beta-test. We’re in!

 March 11th 2014

Robust Scheduling Engine

Platform Scheduler supports Ecological Momentary Assessment - the gold standard in data collection methodology.

 February 11th, 2014

Bayer MaterialScience Approaches tACC

Bayer MaterialScience approaches tACC about developing a new type of Employee Engagement Program for younger employees. They want something sleek and modern.

 February 9th 2014

Ember Beautification

Experiences sent from the H2H Platform are converted to Ember Web Applications - flexible, powerful and beautiful.

 January 14th 2014

The Platform Finds a Home

We had developed a small platform to test our idea - it worked and people were interested! But, it wasn’t scaleable and it wasn’t flexible. So, we rewrote the initial code in Ruby, parked it in the Amazon Cloud, and set up shop on GitHub for source code management.

 January 1st 2014

A Platform Designed

Our platform had to do more than ask questions. It had to easily facilitate the creation, management, and delivery of content. It had to collect and analyze data. It had to provide reports and feedback. It had to be powerful. It also had to be engaging. It needed to feel like an interaction with a friend - not a piece of technology. To build bonds, it had to be non-intrusive and convenient. It had to be flexible. It had to learn, and it had to adapt. It had to be beautiful.

 2013-2014

An Idea Formed

It started with 2 years of research, reading, discussions, meetings...and it resulted in an idea: a Human to Human platform that Interacts with people, over time, getting to know them as individuals, as opposed to demographics.

 2012-2013
