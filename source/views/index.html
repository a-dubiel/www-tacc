About tACC

the Affective Computing Company develops software products and services that influence, recognize, interpret, process, and simulate human emotion. But, what does that mean? And, what’s the purpose?

Groups and Teams

The H2H Platform builds lasting bonds between groups and teams. This could refer to an employer and employess, a doctor and patiens, a teacher and students - any relationship that relies on continued growth and trust.

Individuals

The H2H Paltform helps individuals understand how their situations affect them. This understanding results in resilient indivduals who make confident decisions and take the right actions.

At the center of it all is the Holy Grail: Self-Awareness. At tACC, our motto is simple.Understand yourself. Feel better. Live better.
